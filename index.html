My story — the heart of Happy Mare

A little context for the person behind the colors, cards, and signs.

Happy Mare is a space for art that feels like a deep breath — soft palettes, bold moments, and pieces that live in everyday life. From digital cards and printable collections to hand‑painted signs for local businesses, this work is about making ordinary spaces feel a little more magical.

This site will grow as I upload more pieces, stories, and offerings. For now, think of it as an evolving portfolio and shop: a place to see what I create, support my work through digital downloads, and connect if you’d like a custom sign or project.
